Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. If you need to stop coaching, reach out before your next billing date so we can handle the change.

Do I need gym access?

Gym access helps, but your plan can be built around the equipment you have available.

Is this beginner friendly?

Yes. Your plan is based on your current experience, schedule, and comfort level.

How are check-ins done?

Check-ins are used to review progress, troubleshoot obstacles, and adjust the plan when needed.

What happens after I purchase?

You will complete onboarding so I can build your plan and get you started with clear next steps.

Is nutrition included?

Online coaching includes nutrition guidance. HYROX prep can focus more on performance, training, or nutrition depending on your goals.
