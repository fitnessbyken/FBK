About Me


Hi, I'm Kenneth — a NASM Certified Personal Trainer and Certified Nutrition Coach passionate about helping people build strength, confidence, and sustainable habits that last. My fitness journey started with my own transformation. After losing over 50 pounds, I learned firsthand how powerful consistency, accountability, and the right guidance can be. That experience is what drives me to help others achieve results they can maintain long-term — not just quick fixes.

I specialize in personal training, nutrition coaching, HYROX training, 1-on-1 coaching, and group fitness. Whether your goal is to lose weight, build strength, improve performance, or simply feel healthier and more confident, my approach is built around creating a plan that works for your lifestyle and goals.

My mission is simple: help you become stronger, healthier, and more confident both inside and outside the gym.
