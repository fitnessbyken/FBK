About Me

I entered the fitness industry after my own weight loss journey, where I lost over 50 pounds with the guidance of a coach. Through both personal experience and coaching, I’ve learned that real progress comes from structure, consistency, accountability, and sustainable habits rather than quick fixes. My focus is helping people build systems that lead to long-term results inside and outside the gym.
