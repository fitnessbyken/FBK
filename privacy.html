Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

Fitness By Ken LLC ("Fitness By Ken," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide through our website, coaching services, and related platforms.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website, submit a contact form, apply for coaching, purchase services, or otherwise interact with our business.

By using our website and services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Voluntarily Provide

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address (if applicable)
  • Fitness goals
  • Exercise history
  • Nutrition information
  • Health and lifestyle information you choose to share
  • Messages and communications submitted through contact forms
  • Information submitted through coaching applications or consultations

Progress Photos and Measurements

Clients may voluntarily submit progress photos, body measurements, and other fitness-related data as part of coaching services.

Progress photos are only collected and used with the client's consent and are used solely for coaching, progress tracking, and support purposes. Fitness By Ken LLC will not publicly share client progress photos without obtaining separate express permission from the client.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the website
  • Referring websites
  • General website usage data

This information helps us improve our website and services.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and coaching applications
  • Provide online coaching and fitness services
  • Create personalized fitness and nutrition recommendations
  • Communicate regarding services, appointments, and coaching support
  • Process payments
  • Improve our website, services, and client experience
  • Send updates, newsletters, and marketing communications (you may opt out at any time)
  • Comply with legal obligations

Coaching Platform

Fitness By Ken LLC utilizes Trainerize to deliver online coaching services.

Information that you provide through the coaching platform may be stored and processed by Trainerize in accordance with its own privacy practices and policies. We encourage users to review Trainerize's privacy policy for additional information.

Payment Processing

Payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Fitness By Ken LLC does not store complete credit card or payment information on its servers. Payment information is collected and processed directly by Stripe subject to Stripe's privacy and security policies.

Lead Forms and Marketing

If you submit a form through our website to request information, schedule a consultation, or apply for coaching, we may use your contact information to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Follow up regarding coaching services
  • Provide information about programs and offers
  • Send occasional marketing communications

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link included in those communications.

Sharing of Information

Fitness By Ken LLC does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers that help us operate our business, including:

  • Trainerize
  • Stripe
  • Email service providers
  • Website hosting providers
  • Analytics providers

These providers are only given access to information necessary to perform their services.

We may also disclose information if required by law or if necessary to protect our rights, property, safety, or legal interests.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Understand visitor behavior
  • Enhance user experience

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, although some website functionality may be affected.

Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites.

Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Request information regarding how your data is used

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Fitness By Ken LLC does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that information has been collected from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
